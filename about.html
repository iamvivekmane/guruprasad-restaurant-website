Welcome To The Hotel Guruprasad
Delicious Food, Friendly Staff, Cozy Atmosphere & Positive Emotions!
Hotel Guruprasad began as a small eatery over two decades ago, built in the year 2000 with a simple vision: to provide authentic Punjabi and Tandoor dishes at pocket-friendly prices. The place garnered immense popularity in no time because it consistently delivered flavourful, high-quality meals at a very reasonable cost. Today, the current leadership has taken forward the founder's legacy to new heights. They have cherished that original vision and transformed the humble eatery into the bustling, beloved establishment it is today. The Guruprasad restaurant currently provides a warm and inviting ambiance with a comfortable seating arrangement for over 50 guests. The restaurant has now added heterogeneity to its menu with a wide range of delectable Chinese cuisine alongside its Indian specialties. This place still holds its roots and swears by serving the most succulent Tandoori dishes in town. It is also constantly exploring and experimenting with other flavours to give new dimensions to the dining experience.
